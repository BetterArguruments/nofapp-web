About the Fapsperiment

The Fapsperiment is an effort to examine whether the abstinence of masturbation has effects on Mood, Energy, Libido, Sex Frequency and Quality.

Participants download the Android App in which they are able to track their mood, energy, libido and sex during their abstinence from masturbation. Additionally, they can take notes and log relapses, should the force be weak in them.

An Open-Source, Open-Data Study

But NofApp is much more than just an App to track mood while abstaining from masturbation. As scientific evidence is generally sparse regarding this topic and peer-reviewed journals are an antiquated system which is deemed to be replaced sometime soon, we hope to deliver a blueprint for (medical) studies in the future. Our concept is geared towards openness regarding every aspect: The Android App and this Website are completely open-source.

On top of that, even the Data is open-access and can publicly be viewed. We believe in complete transparency while respecting the anonymity of Fapstronauts.

Feel Free to Contribute
